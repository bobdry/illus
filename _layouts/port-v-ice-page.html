CHAPTER I

Detour

Starting on the highway from Pittsburgh, the Kingdom family;

Wilson,

Monica,

Lucy

and Max are going home to Cincinatti.

In the middle of nowhere Monica, who is eight months pregnant, informs Wilson they must find a bathroom now.

“Honey, we’re in the middle of nowhere.”

“Find one now or we’re going to have a mess.” Monica assures him.

Luckily there’s a random exit with what looks to be a gas station 3 miles off the highway.

As they drive down a country road that 3 miles seems a lot further.

Wilson remarks, “Gosh that highway sign said 3 miles but it feels like we’ve gone 5 already.” “Oh oh oh” Monica stresses, “I’m about to pop!”

Then around a curve there it is, an old gas station. They pull over.

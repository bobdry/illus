6: Responders

Two hours later, Ralph and his truck are on the scene as well as the Pittsville PD.

“Give us two days to get it fixed, Officer White. We should have it back on the road by, I’d say, Wednesday.” Officer White responds, “Sounds good, Ralph. Keep us posted.”

Monica has managed to keep it together but panic is really starting to set in.

”It doesn’t make any sense, officers. Where could he have gone? He gets out of the van for like five minutes and then disappears? No way. I’m terrified that something bad or someone bad has gotten him.”

Sheriff Grady tries to steady the situation. “Mrs. Kingdom, please try not to worry. Three of our finest officers will stay here and keep searching for Mr. Kingdom as long as it takes. We will find your husband, I can assure you.”

“I hope so.”

Officer White steps in, “Mrs. Kingdom, let’s get you and the kids to the station and go over all the details.”

Sheriff Grady huddles with the other officers, “Look guys, who knows what’s going on here. Do a really good sweep of the area and be back at the station by midnight. Oh yeah, and stop by Chad’s on the way and give him a hard time. I hope to the good Lord he’s hasn’t caused another incident.”

The search begins.

“We’re going to the police station, Max and Lucy, the officers will stay to look for your dad.”

“But Mom! We can’t just leave him out there and hope these dudes find him. That’s crazy!”

“I know, Lucy, I know. This is all crazy and terrifying but we have to trust them and have faith that your dad is ok. He just got lost is all.”

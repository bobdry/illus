Cover

1: Detour

Starting on the highway from Pittsburgh, the Kingdom family,

Wilson,

Monica,

Lucy

and Max are going home to Cincinatti after having spent a lovely holiday weekend with Wilson’s parents.

In the middle of nowhere Monica, who is eight months pregnant, informs Wilson they must find a bathroom now.

“Honey, we’re in the middle of nowhere.”

“Find one now or we’re going to have a mess.” Monica assures him.

Luckily there’s a random exit with what looks to be a gas station 3 miles off the highway.

As they drive down a country road that 3 miles seems a lot further.

Wilson remarks, “Gosh that highway sign said 3 miles but it feels like we’ve gone 5 already.” “Oh oh oh” Monica stresses, “I’m about to pop!”

Then around a curve there it is, an old gas station, Chad’s Gas & Grill. They pull over.

Monica bursts from the family van.

The rest of them make their way inside.

The kids tell each other all the snacks they’ll get

and head straight for the junk food aisle.

Wilson approaches the counter. A scruffy fella greets him. “Hey buddy, what cha need?”

“I’ll take $40 on pump 1 out there plus whatever snacks the kids decide on.”

Wilson laughs a little as he turns to look at Lucy and Max.

Scruffy fella says, “Sure buddy. Hey, got a question for ya.”

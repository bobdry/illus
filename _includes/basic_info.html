ABOUT BOB

I’ve worked in the creative industry for a long time. As an illustrator I’ve drawn for The Izaak Walton League, Nature’s Delight, Campbell Magazine, Corona and Modelo Beers and a few others.


my mug

My illustration style combines the sincerity of hand drawing with the vibrancy of digital color application. I offer clients original art that will mesmerize their audience.

AS FOR INSPIRATION

I’m inspired by our species — how we get along and how we don’t. Being a kid of the 80’s, cartoons, comics and movies had me dreaming big well before everything went digital. Ideally, in the mornings, I'll take my coffee black and a seat at the drafting table. Mind and hand, let the drawing flow.

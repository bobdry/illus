ABOUT BOB

I’ve worked in the creative industry for a long time. As an illustrator I’ve drawn for The Izaak Walton League, Nature’s Delight, Campbell Magazine, Corona and Modelo Beers and a few others.


my mug

My illustration style combines the sincerity of hand drawing with the vibrancy of digital color application. I provide clients with something unique, an original image that catches audiences’ attention.

AS FOR INSPIRATION

I’m inspired by our species, how we get along and how we don’t. Being a kid of the 80’s – cartoons, comics and vhs had me dreaming big well before everything went digital. When I wake up in the morning I'll take a cup of coffee and a seat at the drafting table. Mind and hand, let the inspiration flow.
